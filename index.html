ABOUT VIJAYSETHUPATHI

Vijaya Gurunatha Sethupathi Kalimuthu Born: 16 January 1978 (age 47 years) is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Tamil films. Sethupathi is the recipient of several accolades, including a National Film Award, two Filmfare Awards South and two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
